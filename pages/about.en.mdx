# About this project

This project was started as part of the **Seminar course: Astronomy and Astrophyics** to give students an introduction to **Fourier-Analysis**.

The information was collected, implemented and written by us (two students in the 13th grade).
We appreciate any constructive Feedback about mistakes or improvement.
